Frequently Asked Questions

How do I search for properties?
Use the search bar on the homepage or browse the properties page. You can filter by location, price, property type, and amenities.
How do I book a property?
Click on "Details" for the property you're interested in, then click "Book Now" to proceed with payment.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept credit cards, debit cards, and mobile money payments.
Can I cancel my booking?
Yes, you can cancel your booking up to 24 hours before check-in. Please refer to our cancellation policy for details.
How do I contact customer support?
You can contact us through the contact form on our website or email us at support@propertyhub.com.
