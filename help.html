Help Center

Getting Started

To get started with Cytti Homes, create an account by clicking on "Log In" and then "Create one". Fill in your details and you'll be ready to browse and book properties.

Browsing Properties

Use the search bar to find properties by location or keywords. You can also filter results by price, property type, and amenities using the filter options.

Booking a Property

Click on "Details" to view more information about a property. If you're interested, click "Book Now" to proceed with payment. You'll receive a confirmation email once your booking is complete.

Payment and Security

We accept various payment methods including credit cards and mobile money. All transactions are secured with SSL encryption.

Cancellation Policy

You can cancel your booking up to 24 hours before check-in for a full refund. Cancellations made less than 24 hours before check-in may incur a fee.
